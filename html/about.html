WHY STREETWEAR?

Our Philosophy

Streetwear, isn't a new concept- the concept emerged in 80s, and ever since then there has been no looking back. The popularity of streetwear has been growing by leaps and bounds.

While the luxury collaborations pitch at a higher price point, suggesting an older consumer with more disposable income, it’s the Gen-Z market building the hype of streetwear. Every Thursday, lines of consumers, predominantly teenage boys (some are so young they are accompanied by parents), wait at the Supreme store for the latest drop.

Four college students from Delhi who have always loved fashion- Harshit, Nidhi, Ayush and Rasika set out to touch the lives of people through fashion. Here's what they had to say, "Streetwear always seemed to intrigue us. But why should streetwear be limited just to western styles? "

Our founders decided to bring the essence of the rustic Indian culture to the forefront while at the same time keep the avenues open for a style fusion and a western influence.

Taking inspiration from the streets of India and the world, presenting a brand that suits your style : राह के लिए उपयुक्त पोशाक !

Galipahinana (गलीपहनाना ) - Curating rustic street styles which make you look chic and elegant. Showcasing minimalictic fashion, made for you with love.
